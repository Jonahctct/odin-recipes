Steak Tacos

Easy to make, delicious steak Tacos!

There's nothing quite like a plate full of delicious steak tacos to make any day into a good one! Once you realise how easy it is to make, you may never want to make anything else for dinner again!

They're perfect for entertaining friends and family, too! Just double the recipe and share away! (Or don't, we won't tell anyone) Follow along below to experience a fun, and easy recipe, that will leave your mouth watering.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Flank or Skirt Steak
  • Corn Tortillas or Flour Tortillas
  • Queso Fresco, Panela, or other crumbling mexican cheese
  • Pico de Gallo or Salsa
  • Chopped White Onions
  • Fresh Cilantro
  • Slices of Lime

Steps

  1. Place the steak in a glass or nonreactive baking dish, and marinade for 2-8 using a marinade of your choice. Click here to see what we use.
  2. For best results, preheat an outdoor grill. The oven broiler or a cast-iron can also be used. Place steaks on the grill, season again with salt and pepper. Cook approximately 4-6 minutes per side.
  3. After resting, slice the steaks against the grain, at you desired thickness.
  4. Warm tortillas in skillet or on the grill for about 30 seconds. Stack the tortillas, and wrap in foil to keep warm.
  5. To assemble tacos, add slices of steak, chopped onions, cilantro, pico de gallo and cheese to each tortilla. Finish with a squeeze of fresh lime and enjoy!
